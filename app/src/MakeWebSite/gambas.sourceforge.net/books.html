Books

I'm aware of four books that have been written on Gambas. Notice that they all talk about Gambas 1.0.

Gambas - Programacion Visual Con Software Libre

A spanish book written by Daniel Campos & José Luis Redrejo, with a foreword by your servant.

There is a translation in brazilian portuguese there.

A Beginner's Guide To Gambas

An american book written by John W. Rittinghouse.

Menguasai Gambas - Pemrograman "Visual Basic" Di Linux

It seems to be an indonesian book. Please confirm, I'm not sure!

Gambas WikiBook

A wiki book on Gambas, located at http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Gambas.
